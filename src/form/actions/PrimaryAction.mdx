#### Available actions

<table>
  <tr>
    <th>Action</th>
    <th>Description</th>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td><code>action="submit"</code></td>
    <td>Automatically triggers the submit action for the nearest form in the above hierarchy</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td><code>action="reset"</code></td>
    <td>Automatically resets the fields of the nearest form in the above hierarchy</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td><code>action="clear"</code></td>
    <td>Automatically clears the fields of the nearest form in the above hierarchy</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td><code>action=\{Function}</code></td>
    <td>Performs any specified action</td>
  </tr>
</table>
